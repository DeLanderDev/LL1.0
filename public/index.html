Why a place-based site?

Most of what makes Lee County Lee County is local to us. Every small business or kind neighborly act helps keep a place a place. Our community deserves a place to brainstorm amongst each other and come together.

Local Lee tries to be that. It's small on purpose. It's slow on purpose. And it's open to anyone who'd like to lend a hand.
