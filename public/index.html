Why a place-based site?

Most of what makes Lee County Lee County is local to us. Every small business or kind neighborly act helps keep a place a place. Our community deserves a place to brainstorm amongst each other and come together.

Local Lee tries to be that. We're small, we move slow, and anyone who wants to help is welcome to pitch in.

Lee County sits along the Rock River in north-central Illinois. The county seat is Dixon (Petunia City to its neighbors), and the rest of the county is a mix of small towns and farm country: Amboy, Ashton, Compton, Franklin Grove, Lee Center, Paw Paw, Sublette, West Brooklyn, Harmon, Nelson, Steward, and the open fields in between. We try to cover the whole of it.
