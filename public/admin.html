Bulk import businesses from a CSV

Upload a CSV with one row per business. Every imported row lands here for you to review and approve one at a time - nothing is added to the directory until you click Approve. Rows that look like an existing listing are flagged as conflicts and shown side-by-side. Existing listings are never overwritten unless you explicitly choose "Use uploaded info".

CSV columns (header row required): name, category, town, description, address, phone, email, website, hours. name and town are required on each row. Download a starter template.

To review

Click Approve to add to the directory. Click Reject to drop the row.

Brand mark

This image appears in the header on every page (and in the footer of social previews). PNG, JPEG, WebP, GIF, or SVG, up to 8 MB. The server automatically resizes raster images down to 512 × 512 and recompresses them, so a high-resolution original is fine - it won't slow down page loads.
